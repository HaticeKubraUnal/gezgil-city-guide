Manavgat Waterfall


Manavgat Waterfall is a natural beauty formed by the Manavgat River, which originates from the Taurus Mountains. The water, which falls from a height of approximately 4-5 meters, spreads over a wide area and offers a peaceful environment to visitors. There are walking paths, tea gardens and viewing areas around it. It provides both a relaxing and entertaining experience with activities such as photography, nature walks and boat tours. It is a popular spot for those who want to cool off, especially in the summer months.

Tazı Canyon


Although Tazı Canyon has long been known by locals living in the region, it was discovered by chance by travelers in 2017. Tazı Canyon is located within the borders of Köprülü Canyon National Park and stands out with its 200–400 meter high cliffs. Ideal for its unique view, nature walks and photography, the canyon has a fascinating atmosphere, especially at sunrise and sunset. It is a unique destination for those looking for a peaceful getaway surrounded by nature.

Adamkayalar


Adamkayalar is an interesting natural wonder consisting of natural rock formations resembling human silhouettes. These rocks, shaped by wind and water, are a peaceful and impressive discovery spot, ideal for nature walks and photography.

Manavgat River


The Manavgat River, rising from the Taurus Mountains and flowing into the Mediterranean, is one of the most important natural resources of Manavgat with its natural beauty, water sports opportunities and fertile environment. It offers its visitors both a relaxing and entertaining experience with boat tours, rafting and nature walks.

Tilkiler Cave


Tilkiler Cave is a cave that draws attention with its natural stalactites and stalagmites, and attracts the attention of nature lovers with its cool structure and mysterious atmosphere. This cave, which preserves its natural structure, is a quiet but impressive stop for those looking for exploration and adventure. It is also the 3rd longest cave in Türkiye with a length of 7036 meters.

Titreyen Lake


Titreyen Lake offers a "trembling" image with the ripples on its surface. Flamingos, herons and many species of water birds live in and around the lake, so the area is an ideal spot for bird watchers. With its rich bird diversity, calm atmosphere and natural beauty, it attracts the attention of nature lovers and photographers. Titreyen Lake is a peaceful area for those looking for a getaway in touch with nature.

Altınbeşik Cave


Altınbeşik Cave is one of the largest caves in Turkey, decorated with stalactites and stalagmites and having an underground lake. The cave, which takes its name from the golden minerals, is a fascinating discovery point for nature lovers and exploration enthusiasts.

Oymapınar Barrage


Oymapınar Dam is an energy and water regulation structure built on the Manavgat River. A popular destination for nature lovers and photographers with boat tours, walks, bird watching and fishing on the lakeside, this dam also attracts attention with its natural beauty.

Gizli Cennet is a tranquil natural paradise surrounded by forests, waterfalls and clear waters. It is a perfect route for visitors exploring nature with nature walks, camping and photography. Gizli Cennet is an ideal resting and exploring area for those seeking peace.

Köprü River - Köprülü Canyon


Köprülü Canyon National Park is one of the longest canyons in Türkiye, with a length of 14 kilometers and depths exceeding 100 meters. This unique natural wonder formed by the Köprüçay River is known for water sports such as rafting and canoeing. The region was declared a national park in 1973 and is an ideal destination for nature lovers with its rich vegetation and wildlife.

Ahmetler Canyon


Ahmetler Canyon is an impressive natural wonder with a length of approximately 12 kilometers. This canyon, formed by the Karpuz Stream fed by the Taurus Mountains, is surrounded by steep cliffs, cool waters and a rich ecosystem. The region is quite suitable for nature activities such as hiking, swimming, camping and photography. The canyon, which stands out with its cool air and natural beauty in the summer months, is an ideal route for those who want to be alone with nature.
