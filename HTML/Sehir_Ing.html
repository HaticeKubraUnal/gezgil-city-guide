Novamall


It is a large shopping mall with more than 120 stores, a cinema, children's playgrounds and a 4,000 m² food and beverage floor. With its central location and wide range of brands, it is an ideal living space for both shopping and social events.

Merkez Külliye Mosque


The largest place of worship in Manavgat, the Merkez Külliye Mosque, was built with inspiration from Ottoman architecture. The mosque serves not only for worship but also as a complex that includes a madrasah, soup kitchen, bathhouse and charitable institutions. In addition, the mosque, which also appeals to foreign visitors with Quran translations in different languages, is one of the notable religious and cultural centers.

Yapay Şelale


Located in the Türkbeleni City Park, the waterfall offers visitors a resting place surrounded by nature with its walking paths, seating areas and green landscape. It is an ideal place for those who want to cool off and take photos, especially in the summer months.

Upside Villa


As the largest upside down house in Türkiye, it offers its visitors a unique experience. When viewed from the outside, this structure, whose roof faces the ground and whose foundation faces the sky, also draws attention in the interior with all the items hanging from the ceiling. Equipped with real and working items, the house offers visitors an environment that challenges their perceptions, is fun and ideal for photo shoots.

Sorgun Mesire Area


Sorgun Picnic Area is a peaceful escape for those who want to spend time in nature among pine forests. Thanks to its shady picnic areas, walking paths and playgrounds for children, it attracts the attention of visitors who want to both relax and do nature activities, especially in the summer months. It is an ideal area for families, nature lovers and photography enthusiasts with its quiet, cool and natural atmosphere.

Side Ancient City


Side Ancient City is famous for its historical ruins dating back to the 7th century BC. Enriched with important structures such as the Temple of Apollo, the Ancient Theater, the Agora and the Ancient Port, this area is a fascinating place to visit with both its historical and natural beauties. Side also draws attention with its lovely beaches on the Mediterranean coast and its friendly atmosphere.

Boğaz Picnic Area


It is a unique natural area located at the junction of the Manavgat River and the Mediterranean. This area creates a calm and peaceful atmosphere with the natural beauties offered by both the river and the sea. Sorgun Bosphorus, which offers an ideal area for picnics with its various trees, grassy areas and wide beach, is also a great place for those who want to do walking, photography and nature observation.

Side Underwater Museum


It is an ideal destination for those who want to scuba dive in the clear waters of the Mediterranean. The largest underwater museum in Europe, Side Underwater Museum offers a rich underwater world filled with ancient statues and wrecks. Diving tours are carried out safely with experienced instructors and all necessary equipment is provided to participants.

Discovery Park


Discovery Park Manavgat offers unforgettable experiences to its visitors with its different sections such as dino park, zoo and aquarium. You can take a historical journey with dinosaur figures in the dino park, observe exotic animals in the zoo and learn about sea creatures in the aquarium.

Central Street


It is one of the liveliest spots in the district and is the center of shopping, dining and social life. This street, full of local shops, stores and cafes, is a popular spot for both locals and tourists. In the past, the street was a neighborhood market and a small shopkeeper, but today it has become a modern city street. It is an important stop for anyone who wants to capture the spirit of Manavgat.

Adventure Park


The park, which is set on an area of ​​approximately 40 acres, offers activities such as rope crossing, rope bridges, wooden bridges and ziplines on various courses among pine trees. These activities allow participants to test their balance and courage skills. The course takes approximately 2.5 hours to complete. It is an ideal destination for those who want to spend a day in touch with nature.

Manavgat River


The riverside is surrounded by parks, cafes and restaurants throughout the city. It is a nice stopover point for those who want to take a walk or relax with the river view. It is also an important destination for those who want to take photos.
